Fore Abbey

Fore Abbey is a fantastic Christian monastery site located just outside Mullingar with four largely intact remains. Students through our fully guided tour will be taught about the Foundation of the Monastery and The 7 wonders of Fore Abbey. Fore is great fun educational excursion for all students and supervisors alike and is highly recommended by Kingdom of Sports.
  • Kingdom of Sports & Fore Abbey Guided tour - €15 per child (free tea/coffee for teachers)
  • Kingdom of Sports & Fore Abbey Guided tour with food - €20 per child (free tea/coffee for teachers)
